Homage to Genius

Infinite Legacy

The premium TIFR Guesthouse facility in the Colaba Campus is proudly named after the legendary Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan.

It contains three individual structural performance tiers of residential units. In addition, the complex offers fully furnished studio formats and comprehensive multi-bedroom apartment spaces directly on site for extended residencies.

Institutional Access: Allocation operations are restricted to credentialed TIFR visitors. Submissions must clear the internal TIFR Datanet workflow module via host faculty sponsorships.
